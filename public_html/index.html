About Us Who are we

Vibgyor Engineering WLL is the interior Design, Fit-out, Trading and contracting company, conceptualized as a provider of wide range of services and products. We are specialized in turnkey interior solutions and is the home to a highly individualistic and talented team to assist our clientele. We achieve this by professionally trying to fulfill their requisites respecting their preferences and we guarantee superlative results. Our success in interior fit out and refurbishment is achieved through innovative design, meticulous planning, seamless integration and application of numerous products and process by working in partnership with the client that gives comprehensive understanding of requirement and project objective.
